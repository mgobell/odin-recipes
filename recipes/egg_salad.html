Egg Salad

Description

This recipe taken from AllRecipes. A recipe to make egg salad used for sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 1/2 Cup Mayonnaise
  • 1/4 Cup Chopped Green Onion
  • 1 tsp yellow mustard
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • Salt and Pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan with cold water. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Cover and let stand in hot water for 10-12 minutes. Peel and chop eggs.
  2. Place chopped eggs in a bowl and stir in mayonnaise, green onion, and mustard.
  3. Season with paprika, salt and pepper.
  4. Stir and serve on your favorite bread or crackers.
